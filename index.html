About Us

Established in 2010, Vasanth Hindi Academy provides central government certificates upon course completion. We have been successfully running for many years, with numerous students attending our examination board annually. Our academy offers excellent coaching, outstanding spoken training, and a friendly approach towards students.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide high-quality education that empowers students to achieve their academic and extra curricular goals.

History

Since our establishment in 2010, we have grown to become a leading institution in Hindi education as well educating Extra curricular activities. Our journey started with a small group of passionate educators and has expanded to accommodate more students each year.

Accreditations and Affiliations

We are accredited by the central government and affiliated with leading educational boards to ensure our programs meet high standards of excellence.

Faculty and Staff

Our dedicated team of educators, led by Mrs. Revathi, Hindi Pandit, M.A., ensures that each student receives personalized attention and high-quality instruction.
